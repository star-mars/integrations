What You Can Do With This Integration

Six powerful capabilities that work together to turn passive registrants into active promoters.

1. Embed the Poster Widget

Add Premagic's poster creation widget directly to your Cvent registration flow. The moment someone registers, they can create and share a personalized event poster — while their excitement is at its peak.

No Coding Required Desktop & Mobile

2. Auto-Generate Personalized Posters

Because we sync all attendee information from Cvent, we automatically create pre-filled posters for every registrant — personalized with their name, company, job title, profile photo, and custom messaging.

No Manual Work No Design Skills Needed

3. Launch Sharing Campaigns

Proactively send poster campaigns to nudge attendees into action — email campaigns with personalized posters attached, reminder sequences, and one-click social sharing CTAs.

Email Campaigns Referral Tracking

4. Complete Attendee Sync

Automatically import your full attendee roster from Cvent — full name, contact info, company, job title, registration type, profile photos, social media profiles, and custom event questions.

Full Roster Import Profile Photos

5. Bi-Directional Data Flow

Premagic syncs valuable data back to your Cvent records — personal gallery URLs for post-event photo access, poster sharing links, and registration IDs for seamless cross-platform tracking.

Gallery URLs Sharing Links

6. AI-Powered Photo Distribution

After the event, attendee profile photos enable instant facial recognition. Event photos are automatically tagged and distributed — every attendee gets their own personalized photo gallery.

Facial Recognition Auto-Tagging
